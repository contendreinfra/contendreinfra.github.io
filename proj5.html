Oswal Industrial Complex - 2

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

Oswal Industrial Complex 2 - The Industrial Park.

The complex is made of 102 sheds, which are long span and column less structure providing more workable space. The park creates jobs for thousands and help in development of India.

The sheds is connected with good source of power and roads along with basic amenities.

PROJECT DETAIL

  • Location: Sonale,Bhiwandi, Maharashtra, India
  • Type: Commercial
  • Total Developed Area: 206,008 Sq.Ft.
  • Period of Completion: 1.5 years
  • Completion Year: 2011
