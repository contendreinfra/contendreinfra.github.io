OUR BUSINESS

OVERVIEW

C ontendre Infratech Private Limited is a construction company founded in the year 2016, adding over a decade of director’s experience in building, creating and transforming the spaces. At Contendre Infratech Private Limited, it is a constant quest and an enduring commitment to ensure that the relationship between these spaces and the lives ensconced in or around, feel truly enriched and satisfied. Enriching lives by creating spaces that perpetuates a constant culture of openness, a lens through which we envision our every project and every minute detail in it – we ensure that our clients experience with us is worthy their time and money.

Our primary business is development of residential, commercial and retail properties and Real estate consultancy. We believe in working with honesty and transparency with clients. Starting off with a small accomplishment at our initial stage of development, we strive to contribute to the Indian Infrastructure Industry. Every accomplishment becomes a foundation for us to do better, to dream bigger, and create a better future.
