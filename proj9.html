Mulund Sanjiv Co-Op Housing Society

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

Redeveloped Residential Project

MAHARERA* Registration Number: P51800004771

Mulund Sanjiv is a redeveloped project in the suburb of Mumbai, transforming 4 storey building into 7 storey building with better ameneties.The building consits of 22 flats, mixture of 1BHK and 1.5BHK. The building also has 2 office spaces at the ground level.

Each home is equiped with internal and external amenities.

  • Floor
  • Kitchen
  • Bathrooms
  • Living & Bedroom
  • Doors & Windows
  • Power Back up
  • General Safety Utils
  • Rainwater Harvesting System
  • Stack Parking System

*MAHARERA is the Maharastra Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, 2016 under which all the new constructions for public are registered to protect home buyers as well as boost the investment in the industry.

PROJECT DETAIL

  • Location: Mulund, Mumbai, Maharashtra, India
  • Type: Residential
  • Total Developed Area: 15,000 Sq.Ft.
  • Period of Completion: 1.1 year
  • Completion Year: 2018
